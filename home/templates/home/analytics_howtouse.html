How to Use the Analytics Platform

How to Generate the Graph

  1. In 'Date Range', add your start and end date. For example: 1st of November 2011 to 1st of December 2011. Note that there is no data beyond May 2015.
  2. In 'Event Selection' select a date from the 'Choose a Date' drop down menu. If drop down menu is blank, that means there is no available data in between the dates you have chosen.
  3. Choose an event listed in the 'Choose an Event' drop down menu. Only after all of this data has been specified will the 'RIC Selection' section appear.
  4. In 'RIC Selection', choose the RICs you wish to appear on the graph. After this, the graph will appear at the top of the page.

How to Use the Graph

The graph will display the data of the RICs chosen over the date range chosen, as well as the average of all of the RICs that are currently being displayed on the graph. Hover the mouse over the graph to show the exact cumulative returns for a particular date for all the RICs and the average being displayed. Clicking on the graph at a particular point will rescale the RICs to 0% on that date, and show the data relative to that date.
